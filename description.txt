My Fortune Block

A highly compatible, modular "Lucky Block" style mod for Luanti. Unlike other lucky block mods, My Fortune Block uses a dynamic scanning engine. It automatically detects items and mobs from your installed mods and incorporates them into the loot pool without any manual configuration. 

Key Features

Dynamic Loot System: Automatically scans all registered items on your server. It finds ores, tools, and rare items from any mod pack you have installed. When yo dig a block items pop out. You could get anything from any mod you have enabled.

Universal Mob Spawner: Detects monsters and animals from mobs_redo, Animalia, and more. Tenplus1 has a great list here - https://forum.luanti.org/viewtopic.php?t=9917

Custom Schematics: Comes with unique structures that can spawn instantly upon digging a block.

Trap System: Includes classic hazards like Pit Traps, Sand Traps, Lava Leaks, and a "Gravity Curse.

"Modder Friendly API: Easy-to-use registration system allows other modders to add their own custom traps or events with just a few lines of code. I am excited to see the wonderful mods people make for my fortune block. Check out example.lua in the mod folder to see how it is done. Add myfortuneblock to the depenences in mod.conf and you are set. 

How to Use
 
Explore the world. You will find them on the surface and underground. Break them to see what happens!

Depends - None

Remember that if you want mobs to spawn you need to install the mobs in the world.
